Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, methods, or products that improve efficiency and solve problems.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs of future generations to meet their own needs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the essential facilities, systems, and services—such as transportation, energy, and communication networks.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving individuals or communities the confidence, resources, and authority to take control of their own lives .

The Vision of Future

The vision of Future Bangladesh 2.0 embraces innovation, sustainability, and digital transformation, fostering economic growth, smart infrastructure, and social empowerment. With advanced technology, green energy, and inclusive development, it aims to build a prosperous, self-reliant, and globally competitive nation.
